Edge Detectors

February 2016

GitHub
  • C

My implementation of Canny and Sobel edge detection algorithms in C.

The Sobel algorithm finds the magnitude of the input image gradient and outputs images where the magnitude passes low and high thresholds. The Canny algorithm determines the gradient magnitude, creates an images of the their peaks, and performs double-thresholding on the peaks to produce the final image of the edges.
